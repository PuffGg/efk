Our Sustainable Farms

Crocodile Sanctuary

State-of-the-art facilities for sustainable crocodile farming, focusing on conservation and ethical practices. Our facility maintains the highest standards of animal welfare while providing valuable research opportunities.

Ostrich Haven

Experience our expansive ostrich farming operation, where these magnificent birds roam freely in a controlled environment. We specialize in sustainable farming practices and educational tourism.

Snail Agriculture Center

Our innovative snail farming facility showcases sustainable practices in heliculture. We combine traditional methods with modern technology to ensure optimal production and environmental preservation.
